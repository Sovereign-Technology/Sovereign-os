Your identity is split into 5 pieces. Any 3 of them can reconstruct your account. Store each piece separately — cloud, USB, trusted people.

About

Sovereign is a self-contained, cryptographic identity and communication system. Your keys never leave your browser. No servers see your private data.

All cryptography runs locally: Ed25519 keys, Keccak-256 entropy, Shamir secret sharing, AES-256-GCM storage.
